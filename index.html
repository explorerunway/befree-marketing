BeFree

Speak your mind. We'll keep the rest organised.

BeFree is a voice-first life assistant. Tap the mic, talk like you'd talk to a friend, and BeFree quietly files what you said as tasks, events, reminders, and grocery items. No noise, no judgement.

Built for people who'd rather think out loud than wrestle with forms.

What it does

Voice in, structure out

Say "remind me to call mum at 5 tomorrow" — get a reminder. Say "buy oat milk and almonds" — get a grocery list. Say "team lunch Thursday at the Italian place" — get an event.

Lives where you do

Syncs with Apple Calendar and Reminders. Geofence reminders fire when you arrive. Works offline; everything reconciles when you're back online.

Yours, not ours

Export your data anytime as JSON or iCal. Delete your account in two taps. We don't sell anything to anyone.
