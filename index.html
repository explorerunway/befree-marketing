BeFree

Speak your mind. We'll keep the rest organised.

Drop it. Keep it. Bring it back.

BeFree is a voice-first life assistant. Tap the mic, talk like you'd talk to a friend, and BeFree quietly files what you said as tasks, events, reminders, grocery items, and notes. No noise, no judgement.

Built for people who'd rather think out loud than wrestle with forms.

What it does

Speak in any order, sorted in seconds

Say "remind me to call mum at 5 tomorrow" — get a reminder. Say "buy oat milk and almonds" — get a grocery list. Say "team lunch Thursday at the Italian place" — get an event. Speech-to-text runs on-device; your voice never leaves your phone.

Lives where you do

Reads from Apple Calendar and Apple Reminders so scheduling speaks the same language as the rest of your phone. Capture from the home-screen mic widget, the lock-screen mic widget, or "Hey Siri, BeFree…" — no need to open the app. Works offline; everything reconciles when you're back online.

Yours, not ours

Export your data anytime as JSON or iCal. Delete your account from inside the app and your data is removed within 30 days. We don't sell anything to anyone, and we don't use your content to train AI models.

Calm by default. Voice stays on-device. No cross-app tracking.
