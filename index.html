BeFree

Your voice inbox for real life.

Drop it. Keep it. Bring it back.

BeFree is a calm intake room for everything on your mind. Tap the mic, say it all in one breath — appointments, groceries, reminders, half-formed ideas — and BeFree quietly files it as tasks, events, reminders, grocery items, and notes. The relief isn't organisation. It's not having to decide where things go.

Built for people whose brain is louder than their to-do app — no boards, no systems, no ceremony.

What it does

Speak in any order, sorted in seconds

Say "remind me to call mum at 5 tomorrow" — get a reminder. Say "buy oat milk and almonds" — get a grocery list. Say "team lunch Thursday at the Italian place" — get an event. Speech-to-text is handled by Apple's speech recognition, and BeFree never stores your audio.

Lives where you do

Reads from Apple Calendar and Apple Reminders so scheduling speaks the same language as the rest of your phone. Capture from the home-screen mic widget, the lock-screen mic widget, or "Hey Siri, BeFree…" — no need to open the app. Works offline; everything reconciles when you're back online.

Yours, not ours

Export your data anytime as JSON or iCal. Delete your account from inside the app and your data is removed within 30 days. We don't sell anything to anyone, and we don't use your content to train AI models.

Calm by default. BeFree never stores your audio. No cross-app tracking.
