Privacy Policy

Last updated 26 April 2026

BeFree (the "App") is built and operated by Vishal Rana ("we", "us"), based in Sydney, Australia. This document explains what we collect, why, who else sees it, and how to get it back or delete it.

Plain-English summary: your stuff is yours. We use trusted third parties to store it and to turn your voice into structured items. We don't sell data and we don't track you across the web.

What we collect

DataWhy
Account identifier (email, or anonymous user ID if you skip sign-in) So your data syncs across your devices and you can sign back in.
What you say into the mic, and what you type To produce tasks, events, reminders, and grocery items.
Tasks, events, reminders, grocery items, projects, tags, chat history you create The product. Stored to your account so it survives across devices.
Calendar events from Apple Calendar (read), and events you ask BeFree to write So scheduling speaks the same language as the rest of your phone.
Apple Reminders (mirror, when you grant access) Optional integration so iOS Reminders shows up alongside BeFree.
Approximate location (only when you opt in to a geofence reminder) To fire reminders like "buy oat milk when I'm near Whole Foods". Used in background only when you enable a geofence; never stored as a location trail.
Subscription state (free / pro) To gate Pro features.
Anonymised crash + usage events (only if you leave Privacy → Sharing on) To find bugs and learn what's confusing. You can turn it off in You → Privacy.

We do not collect: contacts, photos, your full location history, or anything that isn't directly tied to a feature you used.

Who else sees your data

We use the following sub-processors to operate BeFree. Each only sees the slice they need to do their job, and each is contractually bound to keep it confidential.

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with advertisers, data brokers, or any other third party.

Where it lives

Your data is stored on Supabase infrastructure in Sydney, Australia (region ap-southeast-2). Some sub-processors (e.g., Anthropic, Voyage, RevenueCat, Sentry, PostHog) operate from US data centres; the data they see in transit may be processed there.

How long we keep it

For as long as your account exists. When you tap You → Delete account, all of your stored data is permanently removed from our systems within 30 days. AI sub-processors hold short-lived inference logs (typically 30 days) which they purge on their schedule.

Your rights

  • Export — You → Export to JSON or Export to iCal. The exports include everything we have on you that's tied to your account.
  • Delete — You → Delete account. Removes everything tied to your user ID from Supabase, PowerSync, and our sub-processors.
  • Correct — edit any item directly in the app.
  • Revoke — turn off mic, location, calendar, or reminders permission in iOS Settings any time. The app will keep working with whatever you leave on.
  • Opt out of sharing — You → Privacy → Sharing off stops crash reports and analytics.

If you're in the EU, UK, California, or another jurisdiction with explicit data rights (GDPR, CCPA, etc.), the rights above are how we honour them.

Children

BeFree is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from anyone under 13.

Changes to this policy

If we materially change this policy we'll update the date above and surface a notice in the app the next time you open it.
